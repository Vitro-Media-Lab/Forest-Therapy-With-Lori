The Wisdom of the Forest.

Forest therapy is more than a walk in the woods; it is an active dialogue with the natural world. Regular time among the trees is scientifically proven to reduce stress, anxiety, and depression while boosting mood, vitality, and creativity.

The benefits of forest therapy include increased natural killer (NK) cells that boost immune system response. Phytoncides released from trees contribute significantly to this increased immunity and decreased production of stress hormones.

The Alchemist Behind the Journey

Lori Young is a certified Forest Therapy Guide and Reiki Master, devoted to bridging the space between modern overwhelm and ancestral calm. After a childhood rooted in the wild and 15 years serving as a Park Ranger, Lori’s path was shaped by the quiet guidance of nature and the wisdom of the trees. Through her own awakenings in the forest, she was called to share what she had received—ancestral knowledge, deep presence, and a way of remembering.

Now, she guides others back to that same organic connection, where healing unfolds naturally and the forest becomes both a teacher and a home.

"When Reiki energy softens the mind and opens the heart, the forest no longer whispers — it speaks with a clarity the soul can truly hear."
